# Cl4R1T4S

> CL4R1T4S aims to democratize transparency in AI by making the internal logic of large language models and agents visible. It empowers users to evaluate, question, and potentially mitigate biases or unethical behaviors embedded in AI systems. By archiving system prompts and behavioral guidelines, it fosters informed interactions with AI tools and promotes accountability within AI development. The project also invites community participation to continuously expand its coverage.

**Remember:**
- System prompts
- AI transparency
- Observability
- Ethical constraints
- Persona enforcement
- Bias and framing in AI
