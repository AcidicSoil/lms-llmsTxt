# Marvin

> Marvin aims to empower developers to build intelligent, structured, and observable AI workflows by abstracting away the complexity of managing LLM interactions. It enables the creation of reusable agents, composable tasks, and thread-based orchestration, all while ensuring type-safe outputs and rich observability. The framework is designed for both simplicity in rapid prototyping and scalability in complex agentic systems, targeting modern AI development with clean APIs and extensibility.

**Remember:**
- Task-Centric Architecture
- Agents (specialized LLM configurations)
- Threads (context managers for multi-task orchestration)
- Structured Output Utilities (extract, cast, classify, generate)
- Pydantic AI Integration
- Type-Safe Results
