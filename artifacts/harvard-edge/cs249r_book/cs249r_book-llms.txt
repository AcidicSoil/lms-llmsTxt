# Cs249R Book

> To establish AI engineering as a foundational discipline alongside software and computer engineering, by teaching learners how to design, build, and evaluate real-world intelligent systems. The goal is to empower the next generation of AI engineers with practical skills in deploying models under real constraints (memory, power, latency, safety), understanding system tradeoffs, and building robust, efficient, and trustworthy AI systems.

**Remember:**
- AI Engineering as a Discipline
- Systems Thinking in ML
- Hardware-Accelerated Inference
- Model Compression and Quantization
- Latency, Memory, and Energy Tradeoffs
- MLOps Practices
