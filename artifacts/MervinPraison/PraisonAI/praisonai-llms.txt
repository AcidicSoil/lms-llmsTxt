# Praisonai

> The purpose of this project is to provide a comprehensive, up-to-date overview of the latest AI trends expected in 2025. It aims to synthesize insights from current research, industry reports, and expert predictions to deliver actionable intelligence on emerging technologies, market shifts, and innovations that will define the AI landscape in 2025.

**Remember:**
- Multimodal AI
- Agent-Based Systems
- Large Language Models with Reasoning
- AI Safety and Alignment
- Real-World Integration (Healthcare, Education, Autonomous Systems)
- Prompt Engineering and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
