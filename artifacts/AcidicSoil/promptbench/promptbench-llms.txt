# Promptbench

> The purpose of prompt-bench is to systematically compare and evaluate different prompt compositions across multiple LLM providers by running Cartesian products of templates, payloads, and skills. It enables developers and researchers to benchmark prompt effectiveness in a repeatable, atomic manner, capturing structured outputs for analysis. The system also supports interactive TUI-based management, event logging, and configurable composition modes to suit various use cases, from simple variable substitution to complex recursive fusion.

**Remember:**
- Fused Prompt Generator
- Triple-Layer Recursive Fusion (House, Influence, Target)
- Composition Modes (replace_last_line_legacy, marker, append, nested_marker, messages, placeholders)
- Multi-Provider Support (LM Studio, Codex CLI, Gemini CLI)
- Atomic Artifact Capture (composed_system.txt, output.json, inputs.json)
- Event Stream (JSONL for job lifecycle events)
