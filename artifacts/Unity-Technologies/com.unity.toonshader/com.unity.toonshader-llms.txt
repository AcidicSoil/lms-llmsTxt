# Com.Unity.Toonshader

> To provide a modern, pipeline-agnostic toon shader system for creating cel-shaded 3D content in Unity, supporting Built-in, URP, and HDRP render pipelines. It enables artists to apply precise color designations (highlight, base, shading colors) and control lighting effects such as rim light, emission, angel rings, and outlines — all while maintaining compatibility with Unity’s ecosystem and Package Manager.

**Remember:**
- Toon Shading: A stylized rendering technique for cel-shaded 3D graphics
- Three Color Map System: Base Color, 1st Shading Color, 2nd Shading Color for precise control over lighting and shading
- Render Pipeline Compatibility: Support for Built-in, URP, and HDRP pipelines with varying feature availability
- Material Capture (MatCap): Integration of material textures to enhance surface detail in a stylized way
- Highlight Control: Independent adjustment of highlight color from scene lighting
- Rim Light & Angel Ring: Stylistic edge highlights for character definition
