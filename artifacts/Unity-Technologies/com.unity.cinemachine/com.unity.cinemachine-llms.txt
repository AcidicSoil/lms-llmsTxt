# Com.Unity.Cinemachine

> Cinemachine is designed to simplify camera control in Unity by automating complex camera behaviors such as tracking targets, blending between shots, and managing camera composition. It reduces manual scripting and manipulation during development, enabling creators to focus on content rather than camera logic.

**Remember:**
- Smart camera tools for Unity
- Camera tracking and blending
- State-driven camera behavior
- Cinemachine Brain for managing camera priorities
- Confiner2D and other constraints
- Spline-based camera movement
