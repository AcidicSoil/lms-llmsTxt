# Promptwizard

> PromptWizardâ€™s primary purpose is to enhance the performance of large language models through a self-evolving, feedback-driven prompt optimization process. It systematically refines both instructions and in-context examples by leveraging LLM-generated critiques and synthetic data, ensuring task-specific improvements. The framework supports diverse use cases including benchmark datasets (GSM8k, SVAMP, AQUARAT) and custom datasets, making it adaptable for research and real-world applications. Its architecture prioritizes interpretability, performance, and scalability through modular components and configurable hyperparameters.

**Remember:**
- Feedback-driven refinement
- Self-evolving prompts
- Critique and synthesize diverse examples
- Chain-of-Thought (CoT) reasoning
- Task-aware prompt optimization
- In-context learning examples
