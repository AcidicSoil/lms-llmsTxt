# Blender

> Blender is a free and open-source 3D creation suite designed to support the entire 3D pipelineâ€”from modeling and rigging to animation, simulation, rendering, compositing, motion tracking, and video editing. It enables users to create professional-grade 3D content for film, games, virtual reality, and more.

**Remember:**
- Modular architecture with Python scripting
- Open-source development under GPL-3.0
- Support for GPU acceleration and real-time rendering
- Node-based systems for compositing and geometry nodes
- USD (Universal Scene Description) support for interoperability
- Animation, simulation, and physics engines
