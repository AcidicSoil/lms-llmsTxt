# Reactive Resume

> Reactive Resume aims to provide a free, privacy-focused, and customizable platform for individuals to build, edit, and share professional resumes without compromising data ownership or security. The project supports real-time editing, AI-powered assistance, multiple export formats, and full self-hosting capabilities, empowering users to control their resume data entirely. It also encourages community contributions through open documentation and a welcoming contributor model.

**Remember:**
- Real-time resume editing
- Self-hosting with Docker
- AI integration (OpenAI, Gemini, Claude)
- Template-based design system
- Privacy-first architecture
- ORPC for type-safe RPC communication
